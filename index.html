About Me

My name is Jessica Doan. I am an international student who came from Vietnam nine years ago in 2016. I grew up in the beautiful Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam, a city that never sleeps. As a graphic designer, what do I want to say about my art? Growing up, I've always loved to go to crowded places for people-watching. Sitting in the corner of a coffee shop and watching how people interact with each other always bring me inspiration. I believe there is beauty in unusual places, and if we look closely, we will find those small details, which people tend to overlook, have their unique charm.

As someone who has experienced different phases of depression over the years, I've learned to treasure little things in daily life and how one positive thing can help uplift a whole state of mind. Through my artworks, I hope people will find joy and inspiration in the ordinary. By using a vibrant color palette and elaborate typeface and imagery, I want to express my gleeful emotions, those inner thoughts that I can't put into words and spread that buoyant spirit to my viewers. I wish my work could be a bridge that helps people connect with their inner selves and bring out the rapture hidden inside.
